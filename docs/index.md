## IPA Vowel Sounds

1. long a **/eɪ/** 
    - a_e (vowel-consonant-e): same /seɪm/, cake /keɪk/, safe /seɪf/
    - -ai-: rain /reɪn/, paid /peɪd/, faith /feɪθ/
    - ay: say /seɪ/,  play /pleɪ/, way /weɪ/
2. short a **/æ/**
    - -a- (CVC)
3. long e **/i:/** 
4. short e **/ɛ/**
5. long i **/ɑɪ/** 
6. short i **/ɪ/**  
7. long o **/oʊ/**
8. short o **/ɑ/** 
9. long u **/ju:/**
10. short u **/ʌ/**
11. other u **/ʊ/**
12. oo sound **/u:/**
13. aw sound **/ɔ/**
14. oi sound **/ɔɪ/**
15. ow sound **/aʊ/**
